Expert OEM & White Label services for cannabis brands seeking reliable, high-quality gummy production.

Why Choose TPO Wellness for Your Gummies?

The demand for high-quality cannabis edibles continues to grow. Partnering with an experienced manufacturer is crucial for brand success. TPO Wellness combines expertise in gummy formulation and production with state-of-the-art facilities to deliver exceptional products.

  • FDA Approved & GMP Certified Facility: Ensuring the highest standards of safety and quality.
  • Expert Team Trained by USA Cannabis Professionals: International knowledge applied locally.
  • One-Stop Shop: Streamlined R&D, Production, and Packaging.
  • Focus on Quality & Consistency: Delivering reliable products batch after batch.
  • Your Success Partner: Achieve faster speed-to-market and ensure brand consistency.

Gummy Manufacturing Capabilities & Specifications

Our primary focus is crafting premium cannabis-infused gummies tailored to your brand's needs. We offer comprehensive capabilities:

  • Infusion Expertise: Skilled in precisely infusing various cannabinoids including THC, CBD, THCV, and CBN.
  • Flavor Development: Leverage our expertise or collaborate with us to create unique, appealing custom flavors.
  • Formulation Options: Standard offerings include vegan and traditional gelatin-based gummies.

The TPO Manufacturing Process

We offer a streamlined process from concept to completion:

  1. Consultation: Discuss your project requirements, goals, and vision.
  2. Formulation & Sampling: Develop and refine your unique gummy formula, providing samples for approval.
  3. Production: Manufacture your gummies according to agreed specifications in our certified facility.
  4. Quality Assurance: Conduct thorough QA testing throughout and post-production.
  5. Packaging: Package your finished products according to your requirements.
  6. Distribution (Optional): Arrange for delivery via our in-house logistics service.

Logistics & Distribution Service

Simplify your supply chain with TPO Wellness's optional in-house distribution service for products manufactured by us.

  • Service: Reliable delivery direct from our facility.
  • Coverage: Currently serving the greater Bangkok metropolitan area.
