Expert Preroll Manufacturing Services

OEM & White Label solutions for consistent, high-quality standard prerolls, blunts, and cannagars in Thailand.

Why TPO Wellness for Your Prerolls?

Prerolls remain a cornerstone of the cannabis market. Delivering a consistently high-quality product is key to building brand loyalty. TPO Wellness offers reliable production for various preroll types, backed by stringent quality control.

  • FDA Approved & GMP Certified Facility: Manufacturing under the highest safety and quality protocols.
  • USA-Trained Experts: Applying international best practices to ensure product excellence.
  • Focus on Burn Quality, Consistency, Compliance: Precision production for a reliable experience.
  • Build Your Brand: Partner with us for dependable inventory and enhanced brand reputation.

Preroll Production Capabilities & Specifications

We specialize in producing a range of popular preroll formats:

  • Types Offered: Standard Prerolls (with filter/cones), Blunts, and Cannagars.
  • Available Sizes:
    • Standard Cones: 0.35g, 0.5g, 0.7g, 1g
    • Blunts & Cannagars: 1.5g, 2.0g
  • Infusion & Enhancement Options: Elevate your prerolls with Hash Infusion, Rosin Infusion, or external Kief Coating.
  • Cone & Paper Options: Choose from Raw Cones, Vibes Cones, or develop Custom Logo Cones.

The TPO Manufacturing Process

Our efficient process ensures a smooth path from concept to delivery:

  1. Inquiry & Consultation: Define your preroll specifications and project scope.
  2. Strain/Material Confirmation: Finalize cannabis material and components (cones, wraps, etc.).
  3. Production: Execute grinding, filling, infusion (if applicable), and finishing stages.
  4. Quality Control: Perform in-process and final QC checks.
  5. Packaging: Package prerolls according to specifications.
  6. Distribution (Optional): Coordinate delivery through our logistics service.

Logistics & Distribution Service
